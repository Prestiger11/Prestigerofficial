Origami Crane

The origami crane is one of the most known pieces of origami in the world. Here I will teach you how to make it.

You can watch a video here to learn and you can scroll down for specific instructions.

The instructions (you should follow along with the video)

  1. Get a square piece of paper.

  2. Fold it in half both ways.

  3. Scrunch the edges of the paper, then pull together (see video)

  4. Fold the edges of the paper to the center, then repeat on other side

  5. Undo the last step (you made a crease)

  6. Create the bird base (see video)

  7. Fold the sides in to the center of the paper - similer to step 4. Repeat on the other side

  8. Fold the top down to the bottom as far as it can go without ripping it

  9. Then fold a flap from the left side of the crane to the right

  10. Fold the bottom flap up as high as it can go without ripping it

  11. Fold the all of the flaps on the right over to the left

  12. Fold the flap from the bottom as high as it can go without ripping it

  13. Fold 1 flap from the left over to the right, then adjust the wings, head, and tail so it looks relistic

  14. Fold outward a bit to see the crane

Congrats, you have a origami crane
